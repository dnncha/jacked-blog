Questions lifters ask before switching.

What is double progression?

Double progression increases reps within a target range before increasing load. For example, you may build from 6 to 10 reps at one weight, then add load and return to the lower end of the range.

Does Jacked automatically change my program?

No. You control the routine, exercises, rep ranges, and targets. Jacked keeps the recent results and progression context visible so you can make the next decision.

Can I track RIR?

Yes. Jacked supports RIR context alongside weight, reps, rep ranges, and recent performance.

Is progressive overload only about adding weight?

No. Adding reps, improving execution within the same target, and managing weekly training volume can all be part of a progression plan.
